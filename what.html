What is Bridge for?




Everyone in this world is talented and blessed with a unique gift. But many of them are not able to get a good job or create their dream company because there is a gap between what they know and what is needed in the industry.

This program is aimed at bridging that gap so that everyone irrespective of their background, culture, educational status, gender, or age can learn the basics of programming and the fundamentals of business so that they can go ahead and get a good job or start their own company.
